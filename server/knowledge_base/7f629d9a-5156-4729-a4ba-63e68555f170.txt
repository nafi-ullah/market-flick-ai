### 1. Introduction

The business idea is to develop an online tool that generates reel videos from any story prompt. This tool falls under the Information Technology (IT) sector and is intended for a global market. The tool aims to leverage AI and machine learning to automate video creation, catering to content creators, marketers, and businesses seeking efficient video production solutions.

### 2. Market Size Analysis

#### TAM (Total Addressable Market) Estimate
The TAM for online video creation tools, including AI-driven solutions, is substantial given the increasing demand for video content across social media, marketing, and entertainment sectors. The market is expected to grow significantly as more businesses and individuals seek to create engaging video content. #### SAM (Serviceable Available Market) Estimate
The SAM for online reel video generation tools is a subset of the broader video creation market, focusing specifically on tools that can generate short-form video content or reels. This market is driven by the popularity of platforms like Instagram Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts. [Source](https://ai.meta.com/blog/segment-anything-2/)

#### SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market) Estimate
The SOM represents the realistic market share that the business can capture in its initial stages. This would depend on factors such as the tool's unique features, pricing strategy, and marketing efforts. The SOM is likely to be a small percentage of the SAM initially, as the business establishes its presence and differentiates itself from competitors.
