Based on the gathered data, here is a comprehensive market size analysis plan for the business idea of an online tool to generate reel videos from prompts in the IT sector with a global focus:

### 1. Introduction
The business idea involves creating an online tool that generates reel videos from user prompts. This tool targets a global audience, leveraging the growing demand for digital content creation, particularly in the form of short, engaging videos suitable for social media platforms.

### 2. Market Size Analysis

#### TAM (Total Addressable Market)
- **Estimate**: The global video creation tool market was valued at approximately USD 599.8 million in 2023 and is projected to reach USD 1,292.0 million by 2032, with a CAGR of 8.9% (Source: [Straits Research](https://straitsresearch.com/report/video-creation-tool-market/)).
- **Insight**: The broader online video platform market, which includes various video creation and editing tools, was valued at USD 8.09 billion in 2022 and is expected to grow at a CAGR of 20.7% from 2023 to 2030 (Source: [Grand View Research](https://www.grandviewresearch.com/industry-analysis/online-video-platforms-market)).

#### SAM (Serviceable Available Market)
- **Estimate**: The AI video generation platform market, which includes automated video creation tools, was valued at approximately USD 1.5 billion in 2023 and is projected to reach USD 10.2 billion by 2032, growing at a CAGR of 23.7% (Source: [DataIntelo](https://dataintelo.com/report/ai-video-generation-platform-market)).
- **Insight**: This segment specifically targets users interested in automated video generation, including features like script writing and scene generation.

#### SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)
- **Estimate**: The AI video generator market, a subset of the SAM, was valued at USD 544.65 million in 2023 and is expected to grow to USD 2,706.60 million by 2032, with a CAGR of 19.55% (Source: [SNS Insider](https://www.snsinsider.com/reports/ai-video-generator-market-3169)).
- **Insight**: This represents the realistic market share the business can capture initially, focusing on the niche of automated reel video generation.

### 3. Competitor Analysis

#### Competitors
1. **Synthesia**
   - **Description**: A leading AI video generation platform that allows users to create videos using AI avatars.
   - **Valuation**: Not publicly available.
   - **Competitive Advantages**: Offers a wide range of avatars and languages, user-friendly interface.

2. **Lumen5**
   - **Description**: A video creation platform that transforms blog posts into engaging videos.
   - **Valuation**: Not publicly available.
   - **Competitive Advantages**: Strong integration with content management systems, ease of use.
   - **Weaknesses**: Primarily focused on text-to-video conversion, which may not suit all use cases.

3. **Pictory**
   - **Description**: An AI-powered video creation tool that helps create short videos from long-form content.
   - **Valuation**: Not publicly available.
   - **Competitive Advantages**: Specializes in summarizing long content into short videos, suitable for social media.
   - **Weaknesses**: Limited to summarization, may not offer full creative control.
